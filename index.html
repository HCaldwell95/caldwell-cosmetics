About Us

Caldwell Cosmetics offers carefully curated treatments to enhance inner beauty and promote a positive mindset. Led by Rabia, a Nurse with over six years experience working within the NHS, our mission is to redefine beauty through holistic well-being, accentuating individual uniqueness.

Specializing in skin treatments tailored to your radiance, we provide a serene environment adorned in calming hues, inviting you on a transformative journey of self-love and confidence.

At Caldwell Cosmetics, we blend expertise with personalized care to nurture both external beauty and overall well-being, celebrating simplicity and helping you discover your best self.

As we continue to grow, we are excited to announce that we will be expanding our range of treatments to offer you even more options for enhancing your natural beauty and well-being. Our future plans include introducing a variety of new treatments designed to address different aspects of skincare, wellness, and self-care.

illumiFacial®

Discover the Secret to Radiant Skin

This advanced treatment combines cutting-edge light therapy with a specially formulated serum to target and reduce the appearance of pigmentation, redness, and signs of aging. Ideal for all skin types, illumiFacial® helps to refine pores, improve skin texture, and enhance overall radiance.

IPL Hair Reduction

Achieve Silky Smooth Skin

Using Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) technology, this non-invasive procedure targets hair follicles, reducing hair growth and providing long-lasting results. Perfect for all areas of the body, IPL Hair Reduction is effective on a variety of skin tones and hair types. Enjoy the freedom of reduced hair growth and the convenience of fewer shaving and waxing sessions.

Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) Treatments

Revitalize Your Skin with PRP

Unlock the rejuvenating power of your own blood with Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) Treatments. This innovative therapy harnesses the growth factors in your blood to stimulate collagen production and enhance skin healing. PRP Treatments are ideal for improving skin texture, reducing fine lines and wrinkles, and promoting a youthful, radiant complexion.

Cleanse & Facials

Refresh and Renew Your Skin

Indulge in a rejuvenating experience with our Cleanse & Facials, designed to deep cleanse and revitalize your skin. Our professional facials remove impurities, unclog pores, and hydrate your skin, leaving it refreshed and glowing. Tailored to your unique skin type and concerns, each facial includes a thorough cleanse, exfoliation, and a soothing mask to restore your skin's natural balance.
